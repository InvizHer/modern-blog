Artificial Intelligence (AI) is revolutionizing the software development landscape, transforming how developers work and how applications are built. This article explores the profound impact AI is having on software development practices, tools, and methodologies.

Current State of AI in Development

Today's software development practices are increasingly augmented by AI-powered tools and systems. From code completion to automated testing, AI is becoming an integral part of the development workflow.
