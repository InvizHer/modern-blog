General Questions

What is InvizHer?

InvizHer is a personal blog focused on sharing programming knowledge, development resources, and coding tutorials. We aim to make technical content accessible to everyone interested in learning to code.

Is the content free?

Yes! All our content is completely free and accessible to everyone. We believe in making programming education available to all interested learners.

Content & Resources

How often is content updated?

We publish new articles and tutorials regularly, typically 2-3 times per week. The topics cover various programming languages, development tools, and industry best practices.

Can I request specific topics?

Absolutely! You can suggest topics through our contact page. We value community input and try to create content based on reader requests.

Technical Support

How do I report issues?

If you find any issues with the content or website, please use our contact form or reach out through GitHub. We appreciate your feedback and work quickly to address any problems.

Can I contribute content?

While we currently don't accept direct content submissions, you can suggest topics or corrections through our GitHub repository or contact form.
